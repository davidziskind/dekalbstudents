Ways to Help

Our coalition runs entirely on parent volunteers. Here are specific roles where we need help right now.

School Page Lead

Own your school's page on this site. Write the community story, gather data, collect parent quotes, and keep information current. Each of the 30 schools needs one volunteer.

Data Researcher

Help compile, verify, and present enrollment data, utilization figures, and cost projections. Engineering or finance background preferred but not required.

Social Media & Communications

Help draft posts, manage coalition social accounts, and coordinate messaging across parent Facebook groups and Nextdoor communities.

Meeting Organizer

Help coordinate carpool, childcare, and logistics for board meetings to maximize parent attendance at public comment sessions.
