Ashford Park Elementary School

Grades:  K–5
Cluster:  Lakeside
Enrollment:  ~412
Address:  1200 Ashford Park Dr, Atlanta, GA 30319
Closure Proposed — As of early 2026, DeKalb County has proposed closing Ashford Park Elementary as part of the 2025–2026 School Alignment Process. No final board vote has been taken. Your action can still change this outcome.

AboutWhy Ashford Park Matters

Ashford Park Elementary has been a cornerstone of the Brookhaven community since its founding, serving generations of families in one of Atlanta's most vibrant neighborhoods. The school's walkable location, diverse enrollment, and dedicated staff make it an irreplaceable neighborhood institution — not simply a building.
