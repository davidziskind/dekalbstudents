What's At Stake

This isn't just about buildings.
It's about communities.

When a neighborhood school closes, more is lost than classrooms. Decades of relationships, community identity, and educational investment disappear overnight — often without adequate justification.

📚

Student Displacement

Thousands of students face longer bus rides, unfamiliar environments, and disrupted friendships — with no guarantee of improved educational outcomes.

Read the data
🏘️

Community Anchors Lost

Neighborhood schools are community hubs — where parents meet, where local identity lives, where kids walk to school. Closure severs those ties permanently.

Learn more
📊

Flawed Data & Process

DeKalb's SAP criteria relies on enrollment projections and utilization metrics that parent coalitions have documented as misleading and incomplete.
